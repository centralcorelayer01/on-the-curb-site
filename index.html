Process & Structure Clarity

Complete the Investor Access form and you are automatically redirected to our off-market platform. There, you can review structured property opportunities presented with pricing, projections, and supporting data.

Reservation secures the opportunity and removes it from circulation. It confirms your intent to progress and initiates structured deal progression. It is not a legally binding commitment to purchase until exchange of contracts.

Once reserved, we coordinate: Further due diligence, Viewing arrangements, Offer confirmation, Formal presentation to seller, and Progression toward completion.

Completion is handled through appointed solicitors in the standard legal manner. On The Curb oversees progression to maintain clarity.
